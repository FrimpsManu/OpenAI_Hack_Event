About the Event

OpenAI Hacks 2025 is a 48 Hours dynamic hackathon designed for tech enthusiasts, developers, and entrepreneurs to showcase their skills and create innovative solutions to real-world problems.

💻

Cutting-Edge Tech

Work with the latest AI models and technologies to build groundbreaking applications.

👥

Diverse Community

Connect with like-minded individuals from various backgrounds and skill levels.

💡

Problem Solving

Address real-world challenges and develop practical solutions with impact.

🏆

Valuable Prizes

Win recognition and rewards for your innovative ideas and implementations.

Opening Ceremony & Team Formation

📚 Day 1 — 10:30 AM

Workshop: AI Tools & Resources

Hacking Officially Begins

🍴 Day 1 — 6:00 PM

Dinner & Developer Fireside Chat

⚙️ Day 1 — 7:00 PM

Evening Debug Sprint

Collaborate with your team to squash bugs and stabilize your build.

🌙 Day 1 — 8:00 PM

Day 1 Recap & Wrap-up

Review your progress, rest up, and plan for tomorrow’s final stretch.

☀️ Day 2 — 9:00 AM

Hack Continues

Fuel up with breakfast and get back to building!

🛠️ Day 2 — 11:00 AM

Midpoint Check-ins

Check progress with mentors, refine project scope, and ensure demo readiness.

Lunch & Final Q&A

Wrap up major tasks while connecting with mentors for final advice.

⏳ Day 2 — 2:00 PM

Final Push & Submission

🎤 Day 2 — 3:00 PM

Project Demos Begin

Each team presents their work to a panel of judges.

🥁 Day 2 — 5:00 PM

Judging & Deliberation

🏆 Day 2 — 6:30 PM

Awards & Closing Ceremony

Celebrate the top teams and reflect on an amazing hackathon.

👋 Day 2 — 8:00 PM

Event Wrap-up & Goodbyes

Collect your swag, connect on socials, and head home inspired!

    Frequently Asked Questions

    Who can participate?

    Anyone interested in technology, AI, or innovation — whether you're a student, professional, or hobbyist!

    Is there a registration fee?

    No, OpenAI Hacks 2025 is completely free to attend and participate in.

    Can I join without a team?

    Yes! Team formation will take place during the opening session. You’ll have the chance to meet others and form teams.

    What can I build?

    Anything AI-related! Use tools like OpenAI’s API to build projects that solve real-world problems.
